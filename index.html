Tesla Model X

Introduction

Tesla Model X is an all electric luxury crossover vehicle, meant for passenger as well as long off-road travels. It is manufactured by Tesla Inc. and is assembled in the US

History

The prototype for the car was unveiled on February 9 2012. Deliveries for the Model X were scheduled from 2015 onwards. There were delays in deliveries of the car due to problems in falcon wing doors and motor cooling.
